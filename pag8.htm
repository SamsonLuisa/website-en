Past perfect

  • I had arrived
  • You had arrived
  • He had arrived
  • She had arrived
  • We had arrived
  • They had arrived

Folosim trecutul perfect:

Explicații Exemple
pentru a exprima o acțiune care s-a petrecut înaintea unei alte acțiuni trecute sau înaintea unui timp stabilit în trecut By the time I arrived at the cinema, the movie had already started.
pentru a exprima o acțiune care s-a încheiat în trecut și a cărei rezultat a fost vizibil în trecut He had injured his leg, so he had to sit on the bench for many games over the past few months.

Trecutul perfect este folosit de obicei cu expresiile:

  • before
  • after
  • already
  • for
  • since
  • just
  • until
  • when
  • by
  • by the time
  • never
